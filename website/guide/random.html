Random numbers

Random numbers are used internally for Math.random(). They are also used currently for random pivot selection in the Array.prototype.sort() implementation.

The default internal random number generator is xoroshiro128+. Shamir's three-op PRNG is used on low memory targets and when the compiler doesn't have 64-bit types. The generators have reasonable quality for practical applications but are not suitable for serious statistics algorithms or cryptography.

You can replace the internal random number generator using the DUK_USE_GET_RANDOM_DOUBLE config option.
